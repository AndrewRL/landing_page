Features
Templates

Select a schedule from your preferred templates or create an entirely new one from scratch.

Shifts

Add and edit all of the shifts you need for each day. Select how many employees you need for each shift. Do you have the same shift on multiple days? Use recurrence to add that shift to any day of your choice in one click.

Employees

Add your employees. Is someone on vacation? Ill? Terminal? Simply select toggle their availability and that employee will not be added to the schedule.

Relax

Ready? Click GO and each employee will be sent their eligible shifts and the option to select their preference for each one. At a day of your choosing, these preferences are collected and our algorithm renders them into a complete and optimal schedule!
